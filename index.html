A Little About Me

I’m Praveena, a fourth-year Fashion Communication student at NIFT Bengaluru with an eye for clean visuals and a soft spot for product and visual design.

I’ve interned with Sunny Side Up, and I believe every design should have a little attitude, mine just tends to show up in color palettes and perfectly aligned grids. My motto? Make it clever, make it clean, and make it a little bit you.
